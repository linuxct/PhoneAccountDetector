PhoneAccount Abuse Detector

Simple application to enumerate and detect any application that (ab)uses adding an indefinite amount of PhoneAccount(s) to Android's Telecom Manager

Get it on GitHub open in new window Get it on Google Play open in new window Get it on F-Droid open in new window

Free as in speech

PhoneAccount Abuse Detector is completely free and open source. Check out the source code, make contributions and be safe.

Privacy friendly

No permissions are (ab)used to log, collect or process any personally identifable user information. Check out the guide for more info :)

Straightforward

PhoneAccount Abuse Detector is available for Android 6.0+ devices and it's dead simple to use, no hidden options & no-nonsense.
